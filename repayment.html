What happens when the loan is repaid?

BANK                     BORROWER
Assets | Liabilities     Assets | Liabilities
Loan 0  | Deposit 0      Deposit 0 | Loan 0

When the borrower repays the loan, both the loan and the deposit disappear. No money is transferred elsewhere. The balance sheets contract symmetrically.

Money is not conserved like physical objects. It is created and destroyed through accounting operations.
