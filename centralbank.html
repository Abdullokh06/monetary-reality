The central bank does not fund the economy

When people say “the central bank prints money”, they usually imagine money flowing into households or firms.

That is not how it works.

What the central bank actually does

Central Bank 
ASSETS              | LIABILITIES
--------------------|--------------------
Government bonds    | Bank reserves
Commercial Bank 
ASSETS              | LIABILITIES
--------------------|--------------------
Reserves +100       | Deposits (unchanged)

The central bank creates reserves. Reserves exist only between banks.

Households and firms cannot hold them.
