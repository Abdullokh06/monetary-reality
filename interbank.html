Interbank transfers do not move money

When you send money to another bank, your deposit does not travel.

BANK A 
                    
Assets        | Liabilities 
--------------|--------------       
Reserves -100 | Deposits -100
BANK B 
                    
Assets        | Liabilities 
--------------|--------------       
Reserves +100 | Deposits +100

The central bank moves reserves. Deposits are recreated locally.
